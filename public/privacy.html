Privacy & Data Notice

This is a draft notice for a prototype product. It has not had a formal legal review — get it checked (ideally by a solicitor) before real customer data flows through this app.

Who we are

The Com'mon People Interview Prep Report is a service that generates a personalised interview preparation report from the job description and CV you provide, optionally including a recorded practice answer and a paid coaching add-on.

What we collect

DataWhy we collect it
Your name, email address, and the company/role you're applying forTo personalise your report and pitch, and to keep a record of your agreement to this notice before we generate anything
Your CV and the job description (uploaded file or pasted text)To generate the gap analysis, pitch and tailored questions
Spoken answers to competency questions (audio, then transcribed to text)To draft your STAR answers — audio is sent to our transcription provider and not retained by us afterwards
Whether you've opted in to marketing communicationsThis is a separate, optional tick-box — kept distinct from your agreement to this notice, which is required to use the service. You can withdraw marketing consent at any time.
Email address, and name (if you book a coaching call)To arrange and confirm your coaching session

How long we keep it

  • Uploaded CV/JD files are deleted from our server immediately after the text is extracted.
  • Recorded audio is deleted immediately after transcription.
  • Report content generated for you is not stored after it's returned to your browser, other than what's needed to build your downloadable document in the same request.
  • Your name, email, and record of agreeing to this notice (plus whether you opted in to marketing) are kept so we have evidence of consent — you can ask us to delete this at any time (see "Your rights" below).
  • Coaching booking details (name, email, chosen time) are kept until the session has taken place, plus a short period afterwards for any follow-up.

Who we share data with

We use the following third-party processors to provide the service. None of them are permitted to use your data for their own purposes beyond providing the service to us.

ProcessorWhat they see
Anthropic (Claude API)Your job description and CV text, to generate research, analysis and drafted answers
OpenAIYour spoken answers (for transcription) and a description of the role (to generate cover artwork)
StripePayment details, when payment is enabled — we do not see or store your card details ourselves
Render (hosting)Standard hosting-level access to run the application

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or ask us to delete the personal data we hold about you, and the right to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us if you'd like to exercise any of these. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Security

Uploaded files are size- and type-restricted before they're accepted, and are deleted from our server as soon as they've been processed. Access to hosting and third-party accounts is restricted to those who need it to run the service.

Cookies

This app does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this notice

We may update this notice as the service develops. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
